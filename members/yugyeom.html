ORIGINS ARCHIVE

Born in Namyangju, South Korea, Yugyeom discovered his passion for dance at an early age and developed into one of K-pop's strongest performers before debuting with GOT7.

DANCE ARCHIVE

Widely recognized for his flexibility, control and musicality, Yugyeom became GOT7's performance centerpiece and won admiration throughout the industry for his unique dance style.

MUSIC ARCHIVE

Beyond dance, Yugyeom developed as a songwriter and vocalist, contributing to GOT7's musical evolution while building his own artistic identity.

SOLO CAREER ARCHIVE

Joining AOMG opened a new chapter for Yugyeom, allowing him to explore R&B, hip-hop and contemporary performance through critically praised solo releases.

PERSONALITY ARCHIVE

Despite his towering presence on stage, Yugyeom is known for his playful, humble and warm personality that has made him beloved by both members and fans.
