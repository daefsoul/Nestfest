COME AND GET IT, GOT7! 안녕하세요, GOT7 입니다!

A 7-member self-producing multinational boy group that debuted on January 16, 2014.

NAME ORIGINS

Inspired by god, signifying "seven people who possess luck." Originally GET7, changed for phonetics.

THE "JJAI!" CHANT

"G.O.T. Seven. Jjai!" inherited from JJ Project. Signifies "giving our all."

GALACTIC TIMELINE

ORIGINS

Born in 1994, JAY B helped his parents farm tomatoes in Ilsan. A fan of god since age 7, he became immersed in B-Boying to escape solitude. He won JYP's 5th open audition (tying for 1st with Jinyoung).

MAY 2012

JJ Project debuts with "Bounce," the genesis of the brotherhood.

SEPT 2013

Mark, Jackson, Yugyeom and BamBam appear as JYP trainees on WIN: Who Is Next?.

JAN 16

Official debut with "Girls Girls Girls."

JUNE 19

Comeback with "A".

OCT 22

Japanese debut single released.

NOV 18

First full album "Identify" released.

JULY

Release of "Just Right" — an anthem of self-love.

SEPT

First music show win with "If You Do."

MARCH

Flight Log era begins with "Fly."

APRIL

First world tour FLY — cities across Asia, North America and Europe.

SEPT

Flight Log: Turbulence released. "Hard Carry" becomes a fan favourite.

MARCH

Flight Log: Arrival completes the trilogy. "Never Ever" sweeps music charts.

SEPT

7 FOR 7 — the first true self-produced album showcasing each member's artistry.

DEC

Eyes On You album — project expanding into solo tracks and unit releases.

MARCH

Eyes On You EP released. "Look" gains major digital traction globally.

JULY

DYE mini-album. Members begin branching into more individual creative pursuits.

NOV

Present: YOU — a gift album released in gratitude to iGOT7 for 5 years of support.

JAN

SPINNING TOP: Between Security & Insecurity released. "Eclipse" & "Miracle" lead.

JUS2 sub-unit debut — JAY B and Yugyeom release their R&B-focused mini.

NOV

CALL MY NAME EP — one of their most critically acclaimed projects to date.

NOV

Breath of Love: Last Piece — their final JYP-era full-length. "Breath (Love Poem)" cements their artistry.

DEC

Contracts with JYP Entertainment expire for all seven members after seven years.

JAN

JAY B personally navigates the legal process to receive the full trademark transfer from JYP — an unprecedented move in K-pop history. GOT7 is free to continue independently as a group forever.

MAY

GOT7 self-titled album released — the first independent group record. "ENCORE" becomes the official anthem of reunion.

DEC

NANANA released — closing the year with their signature fun-filled energy.

THROUGHOUT

All seven members thrive in parallel solo careers across multiple labels — music, acting, hosting, and producing.

OCT

AREA 52 — a group project drop amid solo schedules. A reminder that seven always comes first.

JAN

GOT7 releases "FLY AGAIN" as a 9th anniversary gift — all seven voices united.

JAY B enlists for mandatory military service. The countdown to full reunion begins.

GOT7 celebrates their 10th Anniversary. Fans organise massive global projects to honour the decade milestone.

NOV

JAY B and Jinyoung are officially discharged from military service, beginning the full group reunion cycle.

Youngjae and Yugyeom prepare for their enlistment following the release of the "Winter Heptagon" archival project.

MAY

Youngjae and Yugyeom officially enlist for their mandatory military service.

NOV

Youngjae is discharged from military service, followed shortly by preparations for the 2027 full group return.

HISTORIC TRADEMARK TRANSFER

In a move unprecedented in K-pop history, JAY B personally navigated the complex legal process to receive the full trademark transfer from JYP, allowing the group to continue independently. This secures the group's future as a 7-member unit forever.
