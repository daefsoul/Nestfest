THE INTERNATIONAL LINE

"AmeriThaiKong!"

ATK — short for AmeriThaiKong — is a fan-created nickname for GOT7's international rap line: Mark from America, Jackson from Hong Kong, and BamBam from Thailand. Though never officially debuted as a unit, their bond, chemistry, and iconic performances have made them one of the most celebrated trios in K-Pop fandom history.

Unique in K-Pop for being a rap line composed entirely of non-Korean members, ATK brings together three distinct cultures, languages, and styles — yet their synergy on and off stage is undeniable.

THE STORY

THE ORIGIN

A Fan-Born Identity

AmeriThaiKong was never an official unit name from JYP Entertainment — it was coined by fans to celebrate the unique trio of GOT7's international rap line. The nickname caught on so widely that even the members themselves embraced it, using it in interviews, broadcasts, and fan interactions.

THE BOND

Since Trainee Days

Mark, Jackson, and BamBam have been close since their JYP trainee days. Their shared experience as foreigners navigating a foreign industry created a bond that goes beyond the stage. From late-night practices to international schedules, ATK has always had each other's backs.

THE STAGE

Iconic Performances

ATK delivered some of GOT7's most memorable rap stages together — including a legendary MAMA performance that proved their chemistry as a unit. Whether performing in Korean, English, Cantonese, Thai, or Mandarin, the trio's multilingual versatility set them apart from any rap line in K-Pop.

THE STYLE

Three Worlds, One Sound

Each member brings a completely different flavour — Mark's smooth West Coast energy, Jackson's explosive Hong Kong charisma, and BamBam's cutting-edge Thai fashion-forward persona. Together, ATK represents the global face of GOT7 and the international spirit of the Ahgase fandom.

BEYOND GOT7

Solo Chapters, Same Bond

Even as each member pursued their own solo careers — Mark in the US, Jackson building his TEAM WANG empire in China, and BamBam dominating the Thai and Korean markets — ATK's friendship never wavered. Fan-captured moments of the three reuniting, travelling together, and supporting each other's work continue to remind Ahgases that some bonds are unbreakable.
