THE FOCUS ERA

"Hello, we are Jus2!"

A 2-member unit composed of Jay B and Yugyeom from GOT7 — praised as a dream pairing of main vocal and main dancer. The two spent approximately five months devoted to crafting a distinctly personal musical sensibility, with all tracks newly produced specifically for the unit.

"Most of what GOT7 shows tends to be bright and refreshing with powerful choreography. Through Jus2, I wanted to present something more refined and sensuous in both music and performance." — Yugyeom

THE TIMELINE

THE BEGINNING

A Dream Pairing

Jay B and Yugyeom had never once shared a stage together as a unit, even at concerts — often saying "it would be fun if we did something together." The company shared the same vision, and preparations began. Notably, the youngest member Yugyeom serves as the unit's leader.

MARCH 5, 2019

Debut — FOCUS

Jus2 released their first mini-album FOCUS and title track "FOCUS ON ME." Both members participated in writing and composing all 6 tracks. Through the title track, the pair drew attention with restrained yet sexy charisma and a well-balanced performance that showcased a side of GOT7 rarely seen before.

APRIL 10, 2019

FOCUS — Japan Edition

The Japan Edition was released with Japanese-language versions of the Korean album tracks, along with an original Japanese bonus track, 吐息 (Toiki). The first press limited edition became a collector's item for fans.

APRIL — MAY 2019

Showcase Tour — 7 Cities

Jus2 held a showcase tour spanning 7 cities across 11 performances — Macau, Tokyo, Taipei, Osaka, Jakarta, Bangkok, and Singapore. The tour marked the unit's first live performances and cemented their international fanbase.

THE VOICES

JAY B

"I never really had the opportunity to show that tone."

When promoting with GOT7, JB was in charge of the higher vocal range. Through Jus2, he finally showed his preferred mid-to-low register — and the response made him proud.

YUGYEOM

"I hope people come away thinking we're a group that can do it all."

Yugyeom wanted Jus2 to present something more refined and sensuous — both musically and in performance — proving GOT7's range beyond their signature bright and energetic image.
