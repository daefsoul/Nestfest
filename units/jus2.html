JUS2

A dreamlike exploration of performance, emotion and artistry from Jay B and Yugyeom.

ARCHIVE DOSSIER

JUS2 debuted in 2019 as a special GOT7 sub-unit featuring Jay B and Yugyeom. Known for atmospheric R&B sounds, elegant choreography and cinematic visuals, the duo showcased a more mature artistic direction compared to GOT7's group releases. Their debut EP Focus became one of the most distinctive projects within the GOT7 universe.
