Jay B × Jinyoung — The Foundation Era

THE FOUNDATION

"Hello, we are JJ Project!"

A 2-member idol duo under JYP Entertainment, debuting on May 24, 2012. Originally debuting as an independent group, Jay B and Jinyoung later re-debuted as part of GOT7 in 2014 — with JJ Project subsequently incorporated as a unit under the group.

"The complete opposite of how having more members means covering for each other's weaknesses — with us, our weaknesses are fully on display. But I think that's actually what gives us the opportunity to upgrade ourselves." — Jay B

THE TIMELINE

PRE-DEBUT

The Fateful Meeting

Jay B and Jinyoung first met at JYP auditions and were paired together by Park Jin-young himself. Despite initial awkwardness, the two proved to be the best fit — with JYP repeatedly trying to place them in different groups, to no avail.

MAY 24, 2012

Debut — Bounce

JJ Project took their first steps into the music industry with debut single Bounce. Written and composed by Park Jin-young himself, the track blended rock, hip-hop, and electronica into a genre dubbed "Hip-Rock-Tronica" — generating significant buzz for the duo's upbeat energy and dynamic performances.

JANUARY 2014

Re-Debut as GOT7

Following the cancellation of their second album due to JYP's new boy group project, Jay B and Jinyoung returned to trainee life before re-debuting as founding members of GOT7. JJ Project was subsequently incorporated as a unit under GOT7.

JULY 2017

Comeback — Verse 2

Five years after their debut, JJ Project made a comeback with mini-album Verse 2. Both members participated in writing and composing every track. The album topped iTunes charts in 12 regions and ranked 5th on Billboard's 2017 Best K-Pop Albums list.

2017

Tomorrow, Today: Photo Exhibition

To celebrate the release of Verse 2, JJ Project held a free gallery exhibition showcasing photographs they personally took and essays written in their own hand — a deeply personal extension of their artistic identity.
