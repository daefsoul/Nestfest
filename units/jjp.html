The origin story before GOT7. The first constellation formed by Jay B and Jinyoung.

ARCHIVE DOSSIER

JJ Project debuted in 2012 under JYP Entertainment with the single album Bounce. Although their first run was brief, the project laid the foundation for what would later become GOT7. Jay B and Jinyoung would eventually reunite with five additional members, forming one of K-pop's most beloved groups.
